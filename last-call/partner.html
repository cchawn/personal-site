APP DEMO/FLOW

Students would open our app at around 8pm for example. They are then presented with a limited number of deals. These deals are chosen based on some search criteria (type of cuisine, price range, etc.) and their location. These deals will be shown to the students one at a time based on proximety (closest deal is displayed first). The user will have 20 second to either pay for the food through our app (swipe right) or see the next deal (swipe left). If they don't decide within 20 seconds the deal disappears and the next one is displayed. There are a limited number of deals so students must pick a deal fast once they think they have found the best one!
